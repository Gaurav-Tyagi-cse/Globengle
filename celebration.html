Curating Celebrations Across The Globe

Destination Weddings

Take Your Big Day Beyond Borders

A wedding is not just the start of a new journey it's the most cherished chapter in your story. We curate unforgettable destination weddings across the world, designed exclusively for Indian couples who dream beyond borders. From serene beaches and royal palaces to fairytale castles and luxury private islands, we help you choose a setting that mirrors your love story.

Every wedding curated by us reflects a fine balance authentic Indian traditions infused with the elegance of international hospitality. Imagine a mehndi overlooking turquoise waters, pheras framed by historic architecture, or a grand reception hosted in a vineyard estate. We partner with global venues and wedding specialists who understand the scale and significance of Indian celebrations ensuring seamless execution of rituals, catering, décor, entertainment, and guest experiences.

Whether you envision an intimate gathering or a lavish multi-day celebration, our team ensures every moment is flawlessly orchestrated. From guest logistics and personalized itineraries to luxury add-ons like chartered yachts and spa retreats, we transform weddings into once-in-a-lifetime experiences.

Milestone Moments

Celebrating Life's Milestones, Your Way.

Some moments deserve to be celebrated with grandeur not just remembered. At GLOBENGEL, we design milestone celebrations across global destinations for individuals, couples, and families who want to make life's proudest moments unforgettable.

Whether it's a 25th wedding anniversary, a 50th birthday bash, or any landmark achievement, we curate tailor-made celebrations in destinations that match your personality. Think private island getaways with curated entertainment, European vineyard dinners with close family, desert safaris with luxury glamping, or vibrant parties in the world's most cosmopolitan cities.

We go beyond planning we create experiences. Our team adds personalized touches such as theme-based décor, curated menus with global and Indian cuisines, exclusive entertainment, and bespoke luxury elements like yacht cruises, helicopter rides, or private villas. Every celebration is designed to reflect not just the milestone, but also the journey that brought you here.

With seamless arrangements and exceptional attention to detail, we ensure that your milestone becomes a memory etched in everyone's hearts. Because life's milestones deserve more than just a gathering -they deserve a celebration that travels the world with you.

Social Gatherings

Where Togetherness Turns into Timeless Memories

In today's fast-paced world, few things are as precious as reuniting with family and friends. We craft memorable reunions and group getaways that bring loved ones together across generations and geographies. Whether it's cousins meeting after years, a festive family holiday, or a friends' escape, every gathering is designed to feel effortless yet extraordinary.

From luxury villas in Bali, heritage palaces in Rajasthan, Mediterranean cruises, African safaris, to countryside estates in Europe, our destinations offer the perfect backdrop for togetherness. Each journey balances relaxation, adventure, and culture-ensuring there's something for everyone.

Children enjoy theme parks, beach activities, and fun cultural experiences. Parents unwind with fine dining, spa retreats, and nightlife. Grandparents cherish comfortable stays, scenic tours, and leisurely exploration. And as a group, evenings come alive with laughter, shared meals, and traditions that strengthen bonds.

From flights and stays to private transfers, curated experiences, and even custom keepsakes like photo books or videos-we take care of everything. What remains for you is the joy of connection, the warmth of togetherness, and memories that will last for generations.
