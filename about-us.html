Our Story

With over 25 years of hands-on experience in the tourism industry and a business presence since 2019, GLOBENGEL stands as a trusted name in creating journeys that go far beyond travel.

Our foundation was built on one simple belief – travel should be seamless, personalized, and memorable. Since our inception, we have curated thousands of blissful holidays and orchestrated unforgettable client events for families, friends, and businesses.

Our expertise has extended to managing events with true responsibility – from preparing flight tickets and hotel bookings to managing complex collaborations, student relocations, and destination weddings. In every project, our focus remains the same: delivering precision, creativity, and care.

Today, GLOBENGEL continues to open its expertise that inspire – whether it’s a luxury escape, a cultural festival, a corporate incentive, or a once-in-a-lifetime adventure.
