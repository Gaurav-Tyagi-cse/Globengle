Frequently Asked Questions
Question 1: Do Indian travellers need a visa for Japan?
Answer: Yes. Indian passport holders require an eVisa for tourism. GLOBENGEL provides full assistance for the application process.
Question 2: Is Japan vegetarian-friendly for Indian travellers?
Answer: While Japan is largely a seafood- and meat-heavy cuisine, major cities offer vegetarian and Indian food. You'll also find local dishes like vegetable sushi, tempura and udon.
Question 3: What's the ideal trip duration to explore Japan?
Answer: 7 to 10 days is ideal to cover Tokyo, Mt. Fuji, Kyoto, and Osaka. You can add Hiroshima or Hokkaido for extended trips.
Question 4: Is Japan safe for solo or female travellers?
Answer: Yes. Japan is one of the safest countries for solo and female travellers. Clean cities, courteous locals, and efficient public transport make navigation easy.
Question 5: What should I pack for a Japan trip?
Answer: Comfortable shoes (lots of walking!), a light jacket, power adapters (Type A/B plugs), and socks (shoes are often removed indoors).
Question 6: Are public transport and Intercity travel easy?
Answer: Extremely! The Japan Rail Pass is popular for foreign tourists and allows unlimited access to high-speed trains (Shinkansen).
Question 7: When is cherry blossom season in Japan?
Answer: Late March to early April in most parts of Japan, but it can vary by region.
Question 8: Is tipping common in Japan?
Answer: No. Tipping is not expected and can even be considered rude. Exceptional service is already built into their culture.
